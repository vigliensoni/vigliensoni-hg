Clastic Music in México

September 22–23, 2022. Morelia and CDMX, México.

I’m happy to announce that on September 23rd, I’ll be performing at Visiones Sonoras 18, the Festival Internacional de Música y Nuevas Tecnologías 2022 in Morelia, México. The festival is organized by CMMAS, the Centro Mexicano para la Música y las Artes Sonoras.

On the 24th, I’ll be performing at 316Centro alongside fellow artists CNDSD, Iván Abreu, and Turning Torso.
