Clastic music web application (2022)

The Clastic Music web app is an interactive application where images are rendered in real time and react to music and human input. The app is result of a collaboration between visual artist Eduardo Pérez and myself.

Scintillation (2022)

Scintillation is the result of a first time collaboration between CMD and vigliensoni. It explores our joint desires for fast-paced rhythms, propulsive basslines, and affective melodies.
